VOCABIFY SCHEMA V3 - GIAI THICH THUC THE, THUOC TINH, QUAN HE

==================================================
0) THAY DOI THEO YEU CAU MOI
==================================================
- Bo module level_test.
- Cau truc hoc chinh: Level -> Topic -> Lesson -> Exercise.
- User hoan thanh het lesson trong topic/level thi unlock level tiep theo.
- Lesson ho tro nhieu dang bai:
  1) en_to_vi_choice  (Tieng Anh -> chon nghia Tieng Viet)
  2) vi_to_en_choice  (Tieng Viet -> chon tu Tieng Anh)
  3) sentence_order   (Sap xep cau)
  4) audio_choice     (Nghe audio va chon dap an)


==================================================
1) ROLES + USERS
==================================================
roles:
- Danh muc vai tro (admin, learner...).

users:
- Moi user co duy nhat 1 role (users.role_id -> roles.id).
- current_level_id cho biet user dang hoc level nao hien tai.

Y nghia:
- Don gian hoa phan quyen (khong dung nhieu role/user).


==================================================
2) LEVELS -> TOPICS -> LESSONS -> VOCABULARY
==================================================
levels:
- Cac cap do hoc (easy -> advanced), co truong order de sap xep thu tu mo khoa.

topics:
- Moi topic thuoc 1 level.
- Vi du level easy co cac topic: animals, school, clothes...

vocabulary:
- Moi tu thuoc 1 topic.
- Chua thong tin hoc tu: word, phonetic, audio_url, definition_en/vi, meaning_vi...

lessons:
- Moi lesson thuoc 1 topic.
- Vi du topic animals co lesson 1, 2, 3...
- pass_score xac dinh diem can dat de tinh hoan thanh lesson.

lesson_items:
- Gan danh sach tu vung vao lesson.
- Vi du lesson 1 cua animals co 10 tu.


==================================================
3) LESSON EXERCISE (phan ban vua hoi)
==================================================
lesson_exercises:
- Moi bai tap trong lesson la 1 record.
- Truong quan trong:
  - exercise_type: loai cau hoi
  - prompt_text/prompt_json: noi dung cau hoi
  - audio_url: dung cho dang nghe
  - correct_answer_text/correct_answer_json: dap an dung
  - vocabulary_id: lien ket tu vung (neu can)

lesson_exercise_options:
- Lua chon dap an cho dang choice.
- Dung cho:
  - en_to_vi_choice
  - vi_to_en_choice
  - audio_choice
- Với sentence_order co the khong can options, dap an dung luu o correct_answer_json.


==================================================
4) LESSON ATTEMPT / ANSWER
==================================================
lesson_attempts:
- Moi lan user vao hoc/lam mot lesson la 1 attempt.
- Luu score, max_score, accuracy_percent, is_passed.

lesson_attempt_answers:
- Chi tiet tra loi tung exercise trong 1 attempt.
- answer_text/answer_json dung cho cau tra loi tu do hoac sap xep cau.
- selected_option_id dung cho cau hoi chon dap an.

Y nghia:
- Co du lich su hoc chi tiet de thong ke va cai thien bai hoc.


==================================================
5) PLACEMENT TEST (giu lai, 1 lan duy nhat cho new user)
==================================================
placement_questions
placement_options
placement_attempts
placement_attempt_answers

Rule:
- UNIQUE(user_id) tren placement_attempts => moi user chi lam 1 lan.
- Sau placement, assigned_level_id duoc gan vao users.current_level_id.


==================================================
6) PROGRESS (quan trong)
==================================================
user_vocab_progress:
- Theo doi tung tu user da hoc:
  - status, dung/sai, lan on cuoi...

user_lesson_progress:
- Tong hop theo lesson:
  - best_score, so lan lam, status completed hay chua.

user_topic_progress:
- Tong hop theo topic:
  - total_lessons, completed_lessons, completion_percent.

user_level_progress:
- Tong hop theo level:
  - total_topics, completed_topics, completion_percent.
  - is_unlocked / is_completed.

Y nghia mo khoa level:
- Khi user hoan thanh tat ca topic cua level hien tai (user_level_progress.is_completed = 1),
  backend unlock level tiep theo:
  - set user_level_progress.is_unlocked = 1 cho level ke tiep
  - cap nhat users.current_level_id (neu ban muon auto-chuyen).


==================================================
7) TOM TAT QUAN HE CHINH
==================================================
- roles (1) -> (N) users
- levels (1) -> (N) topics
- topics (1) -> (N) vocabulary
- topics (1) -> (N) lessons
- lessons (1) -> (N) lesson_items
- vocabulary (1) -> (N) lesson_items
- lessons (1) -> (N) lesson_exercises
- lesson_exercises (1) -> (N) lesson_exercise_options
- users (1) -> (N) lesson_attempts
- lesson_attempts (1) -> (N) lesson_attempt_answers
- users (1) -> (N) user_vocab_progress
- users (1) -> (N) user_lesson_progress
- users (1) -> (N) user_topic_progress
- users (1) -> (N) user_level_progress


==================================================
8) VI DU LESSON 10 TU (DUNG Y BAN)
==================================================
Topic: Animals
Lesson 1 co 10 tu: cat, dog, lion, tiger, ...

Co the tao bai tap nhu sau:
- C1 (en_to_vi_choice): "cat" -> chon "con meo"
- C2 (vi_to_en_choice): "con cho" -> chon "dog"
- C3 (audio_choice): nghe /dog/ -> chon "dog"
- C4 (sentence_order): sap xep token thanh cau "The cat is under the table."

Moi cau la 1 record trong lesson_exercises.
Neu la choice thi tao them 4 options trong lesson_exercise_options.
Khi user lam xong:
- Tao lesson_attempts
- Tao lesson_attempt_answers cho tung cau
- Cap nhat user_lesson_progress
- Neu dat pass_score -> status lesson = completed
- Tu do cap nhat user_topic_progress va user_level_progress.
